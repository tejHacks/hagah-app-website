# Hagah — Daily Declarations & Biblical Truths

**Hagah** is a mobile application built with **React Native** that helps users meditate on the Word of God through daily scriptures and positive declarations. Speak life over yourself, strengthen your spirit, and keep the promises of God at the forefront of your day.

## 🚀 Features

*   **Daily Scriptures**: Receive curated biblical verses to meditate on daily.
*   **Positive Declarations**: Empower your life by speaking the Word of God over your situations.
*   **Copy & Share**: Easily copy verses or share declarations with friends and family.
*   **Customizable**: Manage your notification preferences and app settings.

## 🛠 Tech Stack

*   **Framework**: React Native
*   **Language**: TypeScript
*   **Build Tool**: Expo
